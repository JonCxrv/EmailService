Hispanic Heritage Month Experiences

This year the DEI Committee will host three experiences during Hispanic Heritage Month. The goal of these experiences is to broaden our knowledge of the Hispanic culture, recognize our teammates who are Hispanic or LatinX (the broader term for the Latino and Latina people) and create excitement about learning useful Spanish phrases. Join us this week for our final experience - the Food Truck Event. Registration is still available.

Food Truck Event

September 29 from 11:30am-1:00pm
Builders Mutual parking lot

Food is an important part of Hispanic heritage. Join us in the parking lot for lunch to sample authentic Hispanic cuisine from a local food truck and enjoy some live music. Be sure to bring your own chair. Masks and/or social distancing are encouraged.

Dance lessons: Alex Tutwiler (Zumba instructor)
Food Truck: EatSpanglish, www.eatspanglish.com
